# 💼 Business Model – Methane Guardian Ai

## Business Model Type
**Methane Mitra follows a B2B SaaS (Business-to-Business Software as a Service) model.**

We provide a climate intelligence platform to organizations that need methane monitoring, emission insights, and compliance support.

---

## Target Customers
- Oil & Gas Companies  
- Government Pollution Control Boards  
- Environmental Regulators  
- ESG & Sustainability Teams  
- Energy & Industrial Companies  
- Climate NGOs / Environmental Organizations  
- Carbon Accounting & Climate Risk Firms  

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Subscription Plans (Primary Revenue)
Organizations pay monthly or yearly to access the platform.

#### Includes:
- Methane leak detection dashboard  
- Super-emitter hotspot identification  
- Source attribution insights  
- Emission severity analytics  
- Geospatial monitoring maps  
- Alerts and notifications  
- ESG and compliance reports  

#### Plan Structure:
- **Starter Plan** – Small teams / pilot users  
- **Professional Plan** – Mid-sized organizations  
- **Enterprise Plan** – Large corporations / government agencies  

---

### 2. Pay-Per-Analysis / Pay-Per-Scan
For customers who do not want full subscriptions, we offer on-demand usage pricing.

#### Pricing can be based on:
- Per satellite scan  
- Per monitored region  
- Per detected methane event  
- Per generated report  

---

### 3. Enterprise Licensing
Large clients can purchase customized enterprise deployments.

#### Includes:
- Custom dashboards  
- White-labeled solutions  
- Dedicated deployments  
- Advanced analytics access  
- Internal compliance monitoring systems  

---

### 4. ESG & Compliance Reporting (Premium Add-On)
Organizations can pay extra for advanced reporting and compliance services.

#### Includes:
- Automated ESG reports  
- Regulatory-ready documentation  
- Methane reduction performance tracking  
- Emission benchmarking  
- Audit support dashboards  

---

### 5. API & Integration Revenue
We can offer API access for other platforms and enterprise systems.

#### Integration Use Cases:
- Carbon accounting software  
- ESG reporting platforms  
- Industrial monitoring systems  
- Sustainability intelligence tools  

---

## Why This Model Works Globally
- Methane regulations are increasing worldwide  
- Enterprises need faster and scalable leak monitoring  
- Satellite-based monitoring is cost-effective over large regions  
- B2B SaaS creates recurring revenue  
- Enterprise licensing increases high-value deal potential  
- API integrations allow expansion into global climate-tech ecosystems  

---

## Scalability Strategy
- Start with methane super-emitter detection  
- Expand into multi-industry emissions intelligence  
- Add predictive analytics and risk scoring  
- Integrate with ESG and carbon accounting systems  
- Build partnerships with satellite data providers and climate-tech firms
